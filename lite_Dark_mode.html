The funds of Pigeon Education Technology Private Limited have been seized by the Enforcement Directorate for allegedly contravening the foreign exchange management act. The company is alleged to have siphoned off Rs 82.72 crore to China and Hong Kong on the instructions of Chinese director Liu Can. The director and accounts manager of the company have admitted that the payment was made only on the instructions of Can.
